Research

I'm interested in scalable robot learning methods. Currently, I am working under Yunzhu Li on a project to generate robotics data from real-world. Previously, I completed a learning-based eye-head control that could replicate and generate human gaze behaviors. To our best knowledge, this is the first paper to introduce the learning-based approach to eye-head control.

Eye, Robot: Learning Realistic Robot Gaze From Human Motion Data
Under Review, CORL 2025
Yuhang Hu, Yuan Fang, Junlin Lyu, Xiaoying Shen, Lechen Zhang, Jiong Lin, Judah A Goldfeder, Steven Tian, Hod Lipson

Realistic Gaze Transformer (RGT), a Transformer-VAE framework that learns full-head gaze dynamics, including head rotations and eyelid movements, from human motion capture data.
